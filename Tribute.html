Michelle Nicolini

A Pioneer in Womens Combat Arts

Main Achievements:

  • ADCC World Championship 2013
  • IBJJF World Championship x8
  • IBJJF World CHampionship 2010, 2008
  • UAEJJF Abu Dhabi Pro 2012
  • IBJJF European Open 2012
  • IBJJF Pan Championship 2011, 2010, 2008

Background

Born in 1982, this Brazillian started her martial arts training in Capoeira, eventually switching to jiu jitsu. She holds a black belt under Robert Drysdale and is considered one of the most decorated champions in BJJ, retiring from the BJJ circuit in 2016. Currently, she is an MMA figher signed to ONE Championship, ranked #2 in the women's strawweight bracket.
